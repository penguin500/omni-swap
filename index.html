FAQ

Common Questions

Is this safe? Do you have access to my wallet? ▼
No. OmniSwap is fully non-custodial. Your Phantom wallet signs every transaction. We never see or store your private key. You can disconnect at any time.
How does the AI bot work? ▼
The bot scans DexScreener for trending Solana tokens, identifies micro-dips on uptrending coins, buys with tight position sizing, and sells using 8 automated rules (take profit at +3%, stop loss at -2.5%, dump detection, etc). One position at a time, max 90 second hold.
Can I lose money with the bot? ▼
Yes. Trading always carries risk. The bot uses tight stop losses (-2.5%) and fast exits to minimize losses, but no strategy wins 100% of trades. Only trade what you can afford to lose.
What's the swap fee? ▼
Free tier: 1%. Pro: 0.5%. Whale: 0.25%. The fee is included in the Jupiter quote -- you always see exactly how much you'll receive before confirming the swap.
How do I pay for Pro/Whale? ▼
Subscription is paid in SOL directly from your wallet. One transaction, auto-renews monthly. Cancel anytime by not renewing.
What tokens can I swap? ▼
Any token on Solana that Jupiter supports -- 500+ tokens including SOL, USDC, USDT, JUP, BONK, WIF, RAY, and all trending memecoins. You can also paste any mint address.
